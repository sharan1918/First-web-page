Valuable Indian players of the World Cup 2024

Virat Kohli

In the final match held in Barbados, Kohli scored 76 runs off 59 balls, which was pivotal in setting a competitive total for India. Despite a shaky start where India lost early wickets, Kohli anchored the innings, ensuring that India posted a total of 176 runs. His innings included calculated aggression, especially in the latter part of the innings, which helped stabilize the team's performance under pressure

SuryaKumar Yadav

Suryakumar Yadav's contributions to India's T20 World Cup victory were multifaceted, combining his explosive batting with exceptional fielding. His ability to perform under pressure, particularly in the final, solidified his status as a key player for the team. His contributions not only helped India clinch their second T20 World Cup title but also left a lasting impression on fans and cricket enthusiasts alike.

Jasprit Bumrah

Player of the Tournament: Bumrah was awarded the Player of the Tournament for his exceptional performance, highlighting his crucial role in India's success during the event
